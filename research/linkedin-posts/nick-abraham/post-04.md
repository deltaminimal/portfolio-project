### Strategy Overview

When onboarding a client in an unfamiliar vertical, ask three strategic questions to extract the necessary raw material to build a high-converting cold email offer.

### The 5-Step Offer Creation Framework

1. **Analyze Existing Assets**
   - _The Question:_ What does your current go-to-market process look like and what assets are you using?
   - _The Goal:_ Identify existing strategies, content, or processes that are working well and can be leveraged or optimized.

2. **Uncover the "Free Value" Element**
   - _The Question:_ What could you realistically give away for free?
   - _The Goal:_ Pinpoint a low-friction asset—such as a free audit, sample deliverable, or teardown—to serve as the core hook of the cold email offer.

3. **Establish a Risk-Mitigating Guarantee**
   - _The Question:_ Is there any guarantee you can make comfortably?
   - _The Goal:_ Define a comfortable timeline, deliverable, or result that removes risk for the prospect without overcommitting the client's resources.

4. **Pressure-Test the Angle with AI**
   - _The Action:_ Run the answers from steps 1 to 3 through Claude.
   - _The Goal:_ Evaluate if selling "Product X" to "Audience Y" makes sense against current industry problems. Use AI to challenge assumptions and surface overlooked pain points.

5. **Centralize the Message**
   - _The Action:_ Pick the single strongest element discovered (the free asset, the guarantee, or a specific pain point) and build the entire campaign around it.
   - _The Goal:_ Maintain strict focus with exactly one clear message per campaign.
