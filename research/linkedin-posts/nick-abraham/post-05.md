### Strategy Overview

Instead of pitching your ICP directly, use outbound campaigns to build a collaborative referral network with adjacent service providers who already target the same audience from a different angle.

### The Referral System

1. **Targeting & List Building**
   - Target high-ticket offers with a well-defined ICP.
   - _Example:_ A loan broker emailing CPAs, tax advisors, fractional CFOs, and bookkeepers.
   - _Workflow:_ Find one strong example of an adjacent provider $\rightarrow$ Use Discolike to find lookalike companies $\rightarrow$ Use Clay to find right contacts and enrich for emails.

2. **Email Framework & Copy**
   - **Context:** Establish that you serve the same market from a different angle.
   - **Credibility:** Drop your value proposition so they know you are legitimate.
   - **Hook:** Ask if there is an opportunity to build a mutual referral partnership.
   - **CTA:** Pitch a quick introductory call.
   - _Note:_ Reply rates are naturally high because the angle is collaborative rather than intrusive.

3. **Back-End Operations (Critical)**
   Do not let the relationships fade after booking a few initial calls. Build a strict management system:
   - Maintain a centralized running list of confirmed referral partners.
   - Check in with every partner monthly.
   - Share case studies and new offers to stay top of mind.
   - Track which partners send the best leads and highest deal volume.

---

> ### ⚠️ Expectations & Timing
>
> Referral revenue is lumpy and unpredictable month-over-month. Do not run this as your very first outbound campaign. Deploy it to compound your results _after_ your direct core outbound channels are already working.
