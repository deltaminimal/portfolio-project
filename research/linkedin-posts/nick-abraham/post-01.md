### Strategy Overview

Instead of applying directly on LinkedIn, use a cold email campaign to book interviews by reaching out directly to decision-makers.

### Step-by-Step System

1. **Infrastructure Setup**
   - Buy inboxes inside Smartlead using domains related to your name.
   - Set up inboxes under your real name.
   - Let them warm up for 2 weeks.

2. **Lead Generation**
   - Build your target list using Smartlead's lead database.
   - Target your potential boss (e.g., Software Engineers target CTOs; SDRs target Sales Managers/VPs).

3. **Email Copy**
   Keep the copy dead simple:

   > "Hey [first name], I came across your [company] and wanted to ask if you're hiring [your role]."
   >
   > [Include one sentence on your experience and what you've accomplished].
   >
   > "If it makes sense, I'd be happy to share my resume and hop on a quick call."

4. **Campaign Launch & Management**
   - Launch the campaign.
   - Expect 30 to 50 responses per 1,000 emails (3–5% reply rate).
   - Maintain high volume; more interviews lead to more offer letters.
   - Reply to interested leads with your resume.

---

> ### ⚠️ Key Caveat
>
> Cold email only gets you in front of decision-makers; your skills must close the deal. If you face rejection, ask for feedback and bridge the gap.
