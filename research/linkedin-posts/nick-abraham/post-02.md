### Strategy Overview

Managing deliverability at scale requires pivoting away from traditional setups. Implement these 5 critical changes to keep cold emails out of the spam folder.

### The 5 Deliverability Switches

1. **Use Split Testing Over Spintax**
   - Copy gets flagged faster than before. Write multiple entirely unique versions of your copy that say the same thing in different ways.
   - Avoid forcing random words into spintax variables, which are prone to errors and harder to QA at scale. Split tests offer cleaner variance.

2. **Extend the Warm-up Period**
   - Rushing inboxes into active campaigns after only one week burns them early.
   - Spend more time warming up inboxes to directly improve long-term inbox placement.

3. **Monitor Weekly Domain Performance**
   - Watch your reply rates closely to diagnose issues:
     - _Flat, low reply rate:_ Typically indicates a poor lead list.
     - _Declining reply rate on stable volume:_ Indicates a domain health problem.
   - A domain with a sub-1% reply rate that continuously declines week-over-week is about to burn. Pull it before it affects others.

4. **Maintain a Pipeline of Backup Domains**
   - Dead domains must be replaced within days, not weeks.
   - Use API automations (like Hypertide) to instantly find and order similar domains so replacements are always ready.

5. **Target Less Saturated Inboxes**
   - Focus on public emails and valid catch-all data.
   - Use waterfall enrichment across multiple providers to build cleaner lists. Less saturated inboxes yield higher reply rates.
