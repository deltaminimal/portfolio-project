### Strategy Overview

No single sales script works for every market. To optimize outbound success, systematically test direct, meeting-focused frameworks against hyper-short, conversational open-ended questions.

### The Two Core Copy Frameworks

#### 1. The 3Cs Cold Email (Compliment, Case Study, Call to Action)

- **Purpose:** Designed to validate direct market demand and generate scheduled meetings immediately.
- **Deployment:** Always start with this framework first. If prospects are ready to book a call based on a strong case study, capture that intent immediately without adding friction.

#### 2. The One-Sentence Cold Email

- **Purpose:** Highly effective for opening dialogue, generating raw response volume, and initiating a sales conversation (especially on LinkedIn).
- **The Script Formula:**
  > "Hey **[Name]**, are you **[doing thing you sell]** for **[Company Name]**?"
- _Examples:_
  - "Hey Mike, are you doing SEO for Unilever?"
  - "Hey Julie, are you using Amazon Web Services at Adobe?"
- **Operational Requirement:** Prospects responding to one-sentence scripts require heavy sales follow-up. You must pivot to warm calling them immediately or executing a rigorous follow-up cadence to bridge the gap from a text reply to a booked meeting.

### Testing Sequence Matrix

- **Step 1:** Launch the campaign with a **3Cs format** using clean data from verified sources like the ScraperCity Lead Database.
- **Step 2:** Monitor meeting booking rates.
- **Step 3:** If booking rates underperform despite accurate targeting, pivot the copy to the **One-Sentence formula** to maximize reply volume, then convert those replies manually.
