### Strategy Overview

Blaming poor campaign performance on deliverability tech or spam filters masks the true problem: generic, copy-and-paste templates. Because top performers book significantly more meetings than average reps using the exact same channels, the focus must shift toward optimizing copy for human attention—specifically on mobile devices.

### The Mobile-First Data Constraints

To avoid being immediately archived or deleted, write your outreach to accommodate mobile reading habits:

- **Reading Environment:** Prospects are 8x more likely to open an initial cold email on their mobile phone rather than a desktop.
- **Word Count Limits:** High-converting emails stay between **50 to 100 words**. Keeping text under this threshold yields a 63% increase in reply rates compared to messages exceeding 150 words.
- **Length Sweet Spot:** Restrict your entire body copy to just **3 to 4 sentences**.

### The "Own The Inbox" Copywriting Structure

A high-performing mobile email relies on four concise components:

1.  **Trigger (Why now?):** A timely, observational opening line rather than a generic greeting.
2.  **Tension (Why change?):** Highlighting a relevant macro problem or highly specific operational friction point.
3.  **Trust (Why us?):** A brief credibility statement or a peer-group case study reference.
4.  **Call-To-Action (CTA):** A low-friction, interest-based question or a direct, valuable offer.

### Real-World Blueprint: Automated Welding Solutions

> **Subject:** welders
>
> Hi Luke,
>
> **[Trigger]** Curious how much manual rework and touch-ups your MIG welders are doing.
>
> **[Tension]** Not sure if you're seeing this, but welding talent is at an all-time low right now. Not to mention how hard automation is for those trailer parts you're building.
>
> **[Trust]** Our welding engineer, Paul, is working with Hitch manufacturers like CURT to help overcome the welder shortage and produce consistent, high-quality welds.
>
> **[CTA/Offer]** Interested in a quick intro to see how?
>
> Corey
>
> **P.S.** If nothing else, you’ll see how your peers are automating the most difficult welds and dealing with the labor shortage.

---

> ### 💡 Key Takeaway
>
> Mediocre, long-winded outbound copy cannot survive strict modern filtering habits. Shorten your messages, format them to be scanned effortlessly on a small phone screen, and make your value proposition immediately apparent.
